The Women Empowerment Club warmly extends its safe place to anyone who is undergoing any sort of troubles within the campus
It is without any doubt that anyone could go through harrassments of different kinds or uncomformtable situations, or encounter things that are supposed to be reported, and may not find the courage to approach the faculties or other authorities regarding it.
Hence, the club is providing a safe network of people who are working to assist in such cases.
